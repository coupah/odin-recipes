Chorizo Pasta

Mmm yummy slightly crunchy chorizo in pasta

This dish is straighforward and tasty af, but you have to watch how long you bake it

  • one large onion or a number of salad/spring onions
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • a "rope" (?) of chorizo
  • 2-3 tablespoons of mascarpone
  • a bit of smelly blue cheeze
  • a few cherry tomatoes
  • a sweep pepper, any colour
  • pepper, salt
  • penne pasta for 4 persons
  1. Put on large pan of salted water to boil. Upon boiling, add penne for four persons. Set timer 10 minutes.
  2. Slice onions, peel garic, put to one side together. In another container, put sliced chorizo, tomatoes, and peppers.
  3. When pasta is al dente, drain and return to the warm pan. Stir in 2 - 3 good tablespoons of mascarpone til it melts. Add the sliced onions and peeled garlic, salt, pepper.
  4. Heat oven to approx 180C. Add other ingredients (chorizo, tomato, smelly cheese) to the pasta.
  5. Put into ovenproof dish with lid. Bake for approx 30 mins, then remove lid for another 10-20 mins to colour.
  6. It's nice fresh, sprinkled with grated cheese, but actually I've found it's MUCH nicer reheated the next day (the flavours are more intense). I also divide into portions and freeze, making a tasty instant microwave meal.

Spaghetti Carbonara

Mmm tasty plate of spagehetti carbonara

This dish is quick to make, tasty, filling and pretty cheap!

  • one med onion
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • 2-3 rashers bacon
  • a little oil for the pan
  • creme fraiche
  • spaghetti
  • mixed herbs, pepper, salt
  1. Put on large pan of salted water to boil. Upon boiling, add spaghetti for one person. Set timer 10 minutes.
  2. Heat pan with teaspoon of oil, med heat. Chop one medium onion and fry slowly. Chop two cloves garlic and add to the onion once it goes clear.
  3. As the onion and garlic are slowly cooking chop 2-3 rashers of bacon into pieces the size of small postage spamps. As the onion is looking well cooked, push to one side of the pan, turn up the heat, then put the bacon in. Stir bacon to ensure it doesn't stick to the pan.
  4. When the bacon is looking a little crispy, turn heat back to medium, mix everything together for a minute, add herb and pepper, then turn heat off. As it cools, add approx 2 table spooons of creme fraiche. Stir it in.
  5. When stirred in, remove from heat completely. Drain spaghetti and add to pan with sauce. Mix together. Leave to sit for a few minutes before serving.
